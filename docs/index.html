This website is dedicated to the work I conduct at the Song Laboratory at the Texas A&M University Department of Entomology. Documented on this site are all of the projects I have previously or currently am working on, along with documentation of pipeline installation, error correction, and a glossary of bioinformatics terminology for quick reference.

There are links for almost every software and package used on this site if you wish to view the original documentation. For installation and execution, I have tried to provide robust instructions but I am limited somewhat by the operating systems I use and am familiar with (Windows OS, slurm cluster).
